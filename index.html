About Me

Hi there! I am Rajshree Deshmukh.
I am final year Information Design Masters student at National Institute of Designing, Bangalore.
I had completed my bachelors in Computer Science from BITS Pilani, Goa (2014) after which I worked as an iOS platform Developer at IBM MaaS360 for two years.

After being a back-end developer of at IBM, I got interested in designing user centered experience driven by data. That's how I landed in Information Design, where I polished my skills in data visualizations, user research, design thinking & UI/UX. I am excited to apply my combined knowledge of software and design in new domains and challenges of industry.
