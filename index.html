About Me

Hi! I am Rajshree Deshmukh.
I am final year **Information Design** Masters student at **National Institute of Designing, Bangalore**. I had completed my bachelors in **Computer Science** from **BITS Pilani, Goa** (2014) after which I worked as a iOS platform Developer at IBM MaaS360 till 2016.

During my time as a developer, I realized, there are differences between a developer's vs a designer's decisions about how things should work. I believed, that the combination of knowledge about the backend & about user-centric design, is necessary to fill these gaps; which led me to pursure Masters in 'Information Design'.
